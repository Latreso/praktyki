Cultural preparation

As part of cultural preparation we participated in: 30 hours of english lessons, meetings with psychologist and lessons about health and safety. All preparations took place in Technical School of Electronics in Rzeszów.

Swedish lessons

Monday to friday, before practice we took part in swedish lessons during which we learnt basic words and phrases. We communicated in english every day.

Trips

In free time we were traveling and meeting with local youth. First trip was two day trip to Kiruna in Sweden. We visited mine which has one of the most (richest) deposit of iron ore.

Second trip was to Santa Claus town - finnish Rovaniemi. We have taken photo with Santa Claus.

Both in Sweden and Finland we were crossing border of the Arctic Circle. Also, we visited swidish city Happaranda and finnish Kemi.
