About project

Projects EU PO WER based on Erasmus + are vocational practices for 80 students in Sweden, Italy, Portugal and Turkey. Main objective of this project is maintaining and improving high level of vocational education through the continuation, consolidation and expansion of international cooperation of Technical School of Electronics in Rzeszów with European institutions. From November 11 to December 6, 20 students participated in vocational practice in Sweden.


The course of practice

Practices held in training facility Utbildning Nord in Sweden. Students were divided between 4 departments: computer programming, web designing, automatics and creating computer networks.
